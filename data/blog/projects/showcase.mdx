Crane is a production studio that explores new artistic possibilities within the rapidly evolving landscape of technology and media. This showcase unveils the production process of *Electro Asura and AI SUN*, a collaborative project between Crane and Taiwan's media art studio, Aisthesis Savage. Born out of an international collaboration involving Korea, Taiwan, and Japan, this project is a fusion of new media performance incorporating artificial intelligence (AI), exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human perception. The showcase will delve into the challenges faced in experimental approaches and the significance of communication among a collaborative team with diverse backgrounds, sharing the trials and innovations encountered throughout the production process. The showcase content will subsequently be documented on Crane's web channel, Channel Crane.

This work visually represents the evolution of humans into cybernetic Asuras. Through the expansion of the dancer's body using Aisthesis Savage's kinetic devices and sensory augmentation technology, and interaction with the LLM-based AI agent 'SUN', the piece explores the transformation of human perception in the cybernetic age. As the dancer's body is extended by the kinetic device in conjunction with the AI's dialogue, they become a mechanical chimera hybrid. During the exhibition, visitors can interact directly with "SUN," a digital twin virtual entity based on the open-source language model Llama3-405b.
